05 Our work

We’ve already done it — for real.

Featured project

Bhatkawa Tea Industries

A 115-year-old listed tea company in the Dooars (est. 1911).

Every batch of tea was valued by hand, combining five separate tasting reports from brokers and the garden — arriving by email, WhatsApp, PDFs and handwritten notes. We built an AI agent that reads them all, values each lot, learns which expert predicts prices best, and shows exactly which buyers to target.

AI agentEmail · WhatsApp · PDFPrice intelligenceBuyer analysis
The problem

Hours of manual work to price each batch, with no way to know which expert was most accurate.

What we built

One agent that combines every report into a single smart valuation and surfaces the best buyers.

The result

Work that took hours now happens in seconds — sharper pricing, and a clear view of who to sell to.
