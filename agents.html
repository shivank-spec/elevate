01 What our agents do

One agent, one job — done the same way, every day.

Each Elevate agent is a tireless office helper that does one job perfectly — on your real, messy paperwork. You don’t learn an app or type anything in: the agent does the work. Mix and match the ones you need, or ask us for a custom one.

Invoice & bill organiser

Reads a pile of messy invoices and bills — PDFs, photos, scans, WhatsApp forwards — and turns them into one clean, GST-ready table, with the e-invoice and e-way details already filled in.

Payment chaser

Tracks exactly who owes you money and how long it’s overdue, then sends polite, on-time reminders so you get paid faster — without the awkward calls.

Bank & cash reconciler

Matches your bank statement against your bills and invoices, flags anything that doesn’t add up, and keeps your books tallied — so they’re always correct without you ever sitting down to tally them.

Money & cash-flow agent

Turns your records into a plain-English picture of money in, money out, profit and what’s coming up — and answers “can I afford this?” in seconds, no spreadsheets required.

Mistake & anomaly catcher

Quietly watches your numbers and flags the odd ones — a bill paid twice, a price that’s off, a missing entry — and catches them early, before they quietly cost you money.

Report summariser

Turns long daily registers, reports and notes into a few clear lines — so you know what happened without reading ten pages.

Document finder

Ask a question in plain language and get an instant answer pulled straight from years of your own paperwork — and it’ll flag anything that looks odd along the way. No more digging through folders.

Email & enquiry assistant

Sorts the inbox, flags what matters, and drafts ready-to-send replies to routine enquiries — so nothing slips and nobody waits.

Stock & reorder tracker

Keeps an eye on stock levels and warns you before you run low — and can draft the reorder so you never lose a sale.

Wages & payroll helper

Adds up attendance, overtime and advances into a clean monthly wage sheet — fewer errors, no late nights with a calculator.

Price & quote analyst

Pulls together rates, costs and past deals so you can price a job or a batch in seconds — with a clear view of your margin.

GST, compliance & renewals watcher

Remembers every licence, GST filing, e-invoice and renewal date, preps the figures, and nudges you well before each deadline — so you’re never caught out.
